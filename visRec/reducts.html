Recommender system for improving Net Promoter Score (customer loyalty) of a company providing repair services

Decision Reducts for NPS

This matrix diagram visualizes minimal sets of benchmarks (reducts) that determine NPS (per chosen client's dataset).

Each colored cell represents benchmark that was found reduct for the given year and client; darker cells indicate reducts that occurred more frequently.

Use the buttons to choose a client and explore the data.

Built with d3.js.
